United Nations Development Programme

Palestinian, Israeli Dispute

Committee Description

Both Arab Muslims and Jews have laid claims to the “holy lands” in the eastern Mediterranean for centuries, but the modern-day socio-political conflict stems from events that occurred during the early 20th century. After over a century of socio-political tension and disenfranchisement, the modern day actors are wrestling a seemingly irreconcilable situation. Though there are solutions that are clear in theory, the two sides are still deeply divided over how to make it work in practice. The UNDP strives to provide a framework that is logical and that acts as a source of reconciliation for both Israelis and Palestinians.
