JHSSMUN 2023

February 11th, 2023

Developed by the leaders of today, for the leaders of tomorrow

Dear Esteemed Delegates, Personnel & Parents,

It is my great honor to welcome you to the inaugural edition of the Johnston Heights Secondary School Model United Nations Conference. The conference, which will take place in-person at Johnston Heights Secondary School in Surrey, British Columbia, is scheduled for February 11th, 2023.

Given the recent circumstances present in the world, JHSSMUN strives to create an environment with an emphasis on learning and development, as well as continuing to maintain the same quality of discussion and debates consistent with the upper-echelon Model UN conferences. As the United Nations parlayed in their recent public statement, “the world continues to face continued adversities - a health crisis, economic disaster, and human rights emergencies rolled into one unprecedented challenge.” As a result, we continue to witness an alarming spread of hatred and discrimination all over the world. Thus, the primary focus of the Johnston Heights Model United Nations Conference this year is the idea of international mindedness.

The conference will host 4 captivating committees: HCC - Berlin Crisis of 1960, SOCHUM - Uyghur Muslim Disenfranchisement, UNODC - Organ Trafficking and UNDP - Palestine Israel Dispute. We believe that the aforementioned committees offer candidates the ability to debate a wide variety of topics all the while continuing engaging discussion regarding such important global problems.

From my first experience as a delegate debating with others to now, I consider myself lucky to have been granted the valuable opportunity to be the Secretary-General. I have gained a significantly more nuanced understanding of the world and the geopolitical circumstances that are present. JHSSMUN is an excellent environment for students like myself to enhance their soft skills. JHSSMUN firmly believes that every student should have an opportunity to engage in the enriching world of Model United Nations. Together with the secretariat team and staff, we are dedicated to hosting a great and memorable event that is free for all attendees. Thank you for joining us, and we look forward to welcoming you in February.

Best Wishes, Krishna Verma

Secretary-General
