2023 Secretariat Team

Secretary General

Krishna Verma

Krishna Verma is a senior IB student at Johnston Heights Secondary School with an immense passion for debate. As a founder of the JHSSMUN club, Krishna has been running weekly meetings to help delegates prepare for upcoming conferences and practice their public-speaking skills. Krishna is dedicated to not only fostering a sense of global citizenship within the conference, but also to giving back to the community. Other than MUN, Krishna is an avid volunteer with GoodGuysBC, an organization dedicated to ending poverty as well as feeding the homeless in Downtown Eastside. Moreover, Krishna is heavily involved with the Wellness Initiative which is a charity organization that Krishna founded with the goal of helping better the community. Krishna is in every club you can imagine, thus, explaining his lack of free time. In his limited free time, Krishna is likely calling or hanging out with his friends, listening to music, playing games on his PS4 or watching sports. Krishna is jubilant to be the Secretary General and cannot wait for the first-ever JHSSMUN conference.

Irtaza Ahmad is a dedicated and driven individual with a passion for debating and Model United Nations conferences. His involvement in the leadership team of previous MUN conferences has allowed him to develop the skills and experience necessary to become the Director General for the upcoming Johnston Heights Secondary School MUN conference. Chief of Staff

Brian Soh

As a senior IB Diploma Program student attending Johnston Heights Secondary School, Brian is beyond delighted to serve as the Chief of Staff for JHSSMUN 2023. Just a year ago, he was one of the founders of the Johnston Heights Model UN Club and has been coaching students of all grade levels in developing their soft skills and confidence for public speaking. His experiences in Model UN have been full of excitement and fruitful debate which he hopes to bring to the first iteration of JHSSMUN. In his rations of free time away from IB, he can be found training in martial arts as a 2nd Dan Black Belt or pondering “what if he hadn’t joined the IBDP?” Brian encourages all delegates, whether new or experienced, to actively participate in the discussions and negotiations at JHSSMUN 2023.

USG Chief of Committees

Oscar Chen

A senior studying within Johnston Heights Secondary School’s IBDP program, Oscar is ecstatic to be serving as the USG Chief of Committees for the first-ever occurrence of JHSSMUN. Though he only began participation in Model UN last year, with most conferences being held online due to the pandemic, Oscar has garnered a multitude of experiences in various committees, leading to deep understandings of how committees function, and the necessities within a committee and its topic to promote fruitful debate. Oscar is certain that those who participate in JHSSMUN 2023 will discover fascinating committees, the allure of debate, and the same interest in diplomacy and teamwork that he has discovered through Model UN. When he is not studying, Oscar is often working as a lifeguard / coach at Surrey Sports and Leisure Complex, or he is scrolling through manga and webtoons on his phone. During the winter, he is often snowboarding at Cypress or Whistler. Oscar wishes that this February will bring forth the spirit of debate, the diplomatic skills of delegates, and deliver to all delegates a unique Model UN experience to last a lifetime.
