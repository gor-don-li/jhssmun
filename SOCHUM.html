Director

Katie Li

Katie is a Grade 12 IB student at Johnston Heights Secondary and is delighted to be serving as the Director of the Social, Humanitarian, and Cultural Committee (SOCHUM). Upon attending her first conference, she has been drawn by the compelling debates and the fast-paced, energetic environment. Outside of MUN, Katie can be found grinding her coursework, struggling with jazz improvisations on the piano, or undertaking a service project in the community. Katie hopes that JHSSMUN 2023 will cultivate lasting memories and is ecstatic to be a part of the journey.

Chair

Dilara Priyankarage

Dilara is a Grade 11 IB student attending Johnston Heights Secondary, who is very thrilled at the opportunity to be the Chair of the Social, Humanitarian, and Cultural Committee (SOCHUM). She was initially drawn to Model UN because of her interest in a career in politics and the desire for more enriching experiences. From her very first Model UN conference, she connected to the impassioned debates and the lively issues being discussed. Aside from Model UN, Dilara enjoys acting in the Johnston Height's Theatre Company, leading the SOGI club and chairing the school's Eagle Council. She hopes that delegates will be able to connect with these issues and each other, and can't wait to see this engaging debate!
