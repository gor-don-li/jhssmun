Director

Katie Li

Katie is a Grade 12 IB student at Johnston Heights Secondary and is delighted to be serving as the Director of the Social, Humanitarian, and Cultural Committee (SOCHUM). Upon attending her first conference, she has been drawn by the compelling debates and the fast-paced, energetic environment. Outside of MUN, Katie can be found grinding her coursework, struggling with jazz improvisations on the piano, or undertaking a service project in the community. Katie hopes that JHSSMUN 2023 will cultivate lasting memories and is ecstatic to be a part of the journey.
