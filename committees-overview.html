Committees Overview

Welcome to JHSSMUN 2023

Our committee's team has worked diligently to provide 4 committees that offer a varied global perspective. The team has carefully selected topics that have significant global relevance and ramifications. Our diverse committees reflect our commitment to developing internal global consciousness at the high school level. Position Papers are required to be eligible for awards and must be submitted by 11:59 p.m. on February 10th.

Our 4 committees are:

- Historical Crisis Committee (HCC) | Berlin Crisis of 1960

- Social, Humanitarian & Cultural Committee (SOCHUM) | Ughyur Disenfranchisement

- United Nations Office on Drugs & Crime (UNODC) | Organ Trafficking

- United Nations development program (UNDP) | Palestine & Israel Dispute
